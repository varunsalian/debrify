Content Responsibility

Debrify is a media management client.

Debrify helps you connect to services and sources that you configure. It does not host, sell, provide, upload, index, or distribute media content.

1. What Debrify Is

Debrify is a client application for managing media-related workflows across services chosen by the user. These may include debrid providers, WebDAV servers, Stremio addons, Jackett/Prowlarr servers, IPTV playlists, local files, and other user-configured integrations.

2. What Debrify Does Not Provide

Debrify does not provide movies, TV shows, music, torrents, IPTV channels, playlists, Stremio addons, indexers, or WebDAV servers. Debrify does not operate or control third-party services that users choose to connect.

3. Third-Party Services

Third-party services are operated by their respective providers or by the user, not by Debrify. Debrify cannot remove content from debrid providers, WebDAV servers, Stremio addons, Jackett/Prowlarr servers, IPTV providers, hosting providers, or other services that it does not own or operate.

If you believe content available through a third-party service infringes your rights, contact that service, addon, indexer, server operator, or hosting provider directly.

4. Debrify-Controlled Material

Debrify can review material controlled by this project, such as official documentation, screenshots, example configurations, bundled links, project-hosted listings, or remote project configuration.

To report Debrify-controlled material, open an issue on GitHub with the relevant URL, file, or screenshot and a clear explanation of the concern.

5. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the services, plugins, addons, playlists, servers, accounts, credentials, and sources they configure. Only use Debrify with content, services, and sources that you own, created, licensed, or are otherwise authorized to access.
