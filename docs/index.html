Everything You Need

One app to manage all your debrid content

Torrent Search

Search multiple sources with IMDB integration. Filter by quality, size, and seeders. One-click add to any provider.

Built-in Player

Native video playback with resume support, subtitle customization, audio track selection, and gesture controls.

Download Manager

Background downloads with pause and resume. Queue multiple files and track progress in real-time.

Magic TV Mode

Purpose-built Android TV experience with channel mode, D-pad navigation, and a lean-back interface.
