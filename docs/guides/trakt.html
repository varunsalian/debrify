Trakt Guide

Connect Trakt, then keep your watching in sync.

Trakt in Debrify helps you resume what you were watching, browse your lists, and check upcoming episodes. This guide covers setup, home screen surfaces, the Trakt tab, and the calendar view.

Set Up Trakt

Connect Trakt once in Settings, then Debrify can use your playback history, lists, and calendar data across the app.

1

Open Trakt from Settings

Go to Settings and open the Trakt integration. This is where you connect or manage your Trakt account.

Settings Trakt Integration status
Open the Trakt integration from the settings integrations list
2

Finish login and review Trakt settings

If you are not connected yet, tap Login with Trakt. Debrify will show a code on screen. Enter that code at trakt.tv/activate on your phone or computer, then return to Debrify.

Once connected, this page shows the connected state and lets you control Sync Catalog Items. Turn that on if you want Debrify to scrobble playback for addon and catalog content too, not just items opened from the Trakt section.

Connected Login with Trakt Sync Catalog Items
Trakt settings after the account is connected

Trakt on the Home Screen

After setup, Trakt starts surfacing useful items on Home so you can jump back in without opening the Trakt tab first.

3

Use Continue Watching

Home shows Continue Watching rows for Trakt movies and shows. These are based on Trakt playback progress, so you can quickly resume what you were already watching.

Movies Shows Resume progress
Trakt adds continue watching rows to the home screen
4

Use the upcoming card at the top

The large card near the top of Home is your Trakt upcoming surface. Tap it to open the full Trakt Calendar screen and browse upcoming episodes.

If Debrify is actively scrobbling something you are watching right now, the app can prioritize the now-playing surface instead of showing the calendar card.

Upcoming episodes Open calendar Now playing can take priority
Tap the upcoming card to open Trakt Calendar

Browse Trakt in Debrify

The Trakt tab is where you can switch between list types, change movie or show mode, and open items for playback.

5

Open the Trakt section

Use the dedicated Trakt section to browse your synced content. Open any item there and continue with Sources or Play, just like other Debrify content views.

Sources Play Calendar button
Browse Trakt lists directly inside Debrify
6

Pick the list type you want

The first dropdown changes the Trakt feed you are browsing. Use it to switch between personal lists and discovery views.

Continue Watching Watchlist History Collection Trending Popular

You can also browse Custom Lists, Liked Lists, Ratings, Anticipated, Recommendations, or run Search Trakt.

The second dropdown switches between Movie and Show.

Switch list type and then choose movie or show mode

Use Trakt Calendar

Open the calendar from Home or from the Trakt section when you want to check upcoming episodes.

7

Browse your upcoming schedule

Trakt Calendar shows upcoming episode dates in a monthly view. Use it to see what is airing soon and jump into the entries that matter to you.

Monthly view Upcoming episodes Jump from Home or Trakt tab
Use Trakt Calendar to check what is coming next
In short

Connect Trakt once in Settings, use Home for quick resume and upcoming items, then open the Trakt section when you want full list browsing and calendar access.
